Rethinking static training programs

Introducing Movement Session

Andreas Flakstad
December 13, 2015

Workout plans and training programs are static. They remain the same over time and can only provide what the author initially intended. This leads us to continually hunt for new inspiration, new exercises, the right programming. If you're anything like us you have spent hundreds of hours searching for workout plans and training programs.

Now, it's true that improved programming gives improved results and at the highest level of performance a well educated trainer should probably plan every detail for you. But most of us just want to move more, be healthier and have fun in the process. That means spending less time searching for training programs and more time moving our bodies in all sorts of different ways.

Interactive & dynamic workouts

If you're looking for variety in your training, you can do better than planned workouts

Andreas Flakstad
February 3, 2016

Static workouts

Traditional workout plans and training programs are static. They will always present what the author initially intended, and only that. Now if the author is capable he might have created a great workout. But what if an exercise is too hard for you? Or too easy? Or you lack the equipment to do the exercise. Now you're off searching for alternative exercises or for easier or harder variations. And of course, if you don't want to do the same workout the next day you have to go on the hunt again for new inspiration, for new exercises, for the right programming. If you're anything like me you have probably spent hundreds of hours searching for workout plans and training programs.
